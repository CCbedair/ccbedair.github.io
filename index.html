About Me

Currently, I'm a Masters student at Aalto University studing Cloud Computing and Security as part of the SECCLO program with a fouced specialization in Communication Systems .

As a Reseacher , my topics of interest include Networks Infrastructres , Machine Learning , Information security and Cryptography .

Regarding my professional skills, I have solid experience in Full Stack Development as well as DevOps , aquired through my work in the industry. Moreover, having worked on various independant projects, I am also familiar with Deep Learning , through popular frameworks like Tensorflow , with special focus on Pattern Recognition and Natural Language Processing . I have also participated in multiple hackathons/gamejams as Game Development holds a special place in my heart. I have working experience with the most poplar engines: Unity and Blueprints Visual Scripting .

My main passion however is Cyber Security . I constantly take part in challenges and CTFs, sharpening my skills in Pentesting , Reverse Engineering , Steganography and Practical Cryptography .
